Accent primary colors offer a secondary emphasis that complements the brand palette without competing with primary actions.

## Usage

- Use `canvas-accent-primary-soft` for gentle backgrounds on cards or banners.
- Reach for `ink-accent-primary-strong` when you need a vivid highlight that is still distinct from brand ink.
- Borders can lean on `border-accent-primary-muted` for separators or `border-accent-primary-strong` for interactive affordances.

```html
<p class="ink-accent-primary-strong border-accent-primary-muted">
  Accent callouts pair well with neutral canvases.
</p>
```

## Tokens

Semantic slots mirror the brand family with canvas, ink, and border roles plus inverse and intensity variants.

## Examples

Combine accent primary ink with neutral or app canvases to keep secondary emphasis clear.
